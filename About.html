About

Anime&Manga Otaku is a reviewing site where you will see the rating, and score of each Anime, Manga, and even including Light novels. Ranging from Japanese manga, light novel to even Korean manhwa and Chinese manhua.

This is to inspire people to read other genres/themes of manga or people who just started recently a good direction to well struture manga, anime, or lightnovel. By introducing more audience to the anime and manga community this will encourage studios to have more manga anime adaption. Examples of popular manga getting anime adaption such as "Tower of god ", and "high school of god", which are anime that was previously a manhwa.

With the continuing help and support from readers like you manga will continue to get a chance of getting a anime adaption.
